Mars Reference Guide
####################


MARS stands for Multi Angle Reconstruction and Segmentation.
It's goal is to fuse standard quality images of organs taken 
at different angle into a higher resolution image. Then this
image can be more reliable segmented.

The MARS package provides registration, fusion and segmentation
algorithms.
